Our Story

Founded in 1975 by the Harrison family, Golden Nest Poultry Farm began as a small backyard operation with just 50 chickens. What started as a passion project to provide fresh eggs to neighbors has grown into one of the region's most trusted poultry suppliers.

Today, we maintain over 50,000 laying hens across our 50-acre facility, yet we've never lost sight of our founding principles: quality, sustainability, and community.

Every egg that leaves our farm carries the same care and attention that our grandmother put into those first dozen eggs nearly 50 years ago.
